
# Nexus AI Platform - User Operation Manual

Welcome to Nexus AI! This guide will help you navigate the platform to accelerate your development workflow.

## 1. Getting Started
Upon launching the application, you will land on the **Dashboard**. Use the sidebar to navigate between different modules.

## 2. The Process Navigator
This is the heart of the platform, guiding you through the software development lifecycle.

### How to Use:
1.  **Select a Stage:** Click the icons at the top (Analysis, Architecture, etc.) to filter templates.
2.  **Choose a Template:** 
    *   **Official (Blue Badge):** System-verified templates.
    *   **Saved (Amber Badge):** Templates you have bookmarked.
    *   **Community:** Templates shared by other users.
3.  **View Details:** Click a card to see the prompt, input requirements, and expected output.
4.  **Use Prompt:** Click "Use Prompt" to copy it to your clipboard or send it to the Playground.

### Managing Templates:
*   **Create:** Click the **+ Share Template** button top-right. Fill in the details (Title, Stage, Prompt).
*   **Bookmark:** Click the **Bookmark Icon** in the detail modal to pin it to the top of your list.
*   **Delete:** You can delete templates you created by clicking the **Trash Icon** inside the detail modal.

## 3. Prompt Library
A collection of specific, role-based prompts for smaller tasks.

### How to Use:
*   **Search & Filter:** Use the search bar or dropdowns to find prompts for your specific Role (e.g., Backend Dev) or Category (e.g., Debugging).
*   **Feedback:** Open a prompt to read comments.
*   **AI Summary:** If a prompt has many comments, click the **âœ¨ AI Summarize Feedback** button to get a quick overview of what others think.

## 4. Playground
Your coding sandbox.

1.  **Chat:** Type your request (or paste a template) on the left.
2.  **Code:** The AI generates code on the right.
3.  **Run:** Click **Run** to see a live preview of web components.
4.  **Analyze:** Click **Analyze** to get an AI-generated efficiency score for your code.

## 5. Tips & Tricks
*   **Bookmarks sort first:** Save your most-used templates to save scrolling time.
*   **Read the comments:** Before using a new prompt, check the comments to see if others found edge cases.
*   **Be specific:** When creating your own templates, clearly define the "Input Format" so others know how to use it.
